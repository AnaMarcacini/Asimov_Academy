          About

          Brasileirão Série A, also known as Campeonato Brasileiro Série A, Brasileirão, is a professional football league in Brazil for men.

          There are overall 20 teams that compete for the title every year.

          The current holder of the title is Palmeiras and the team that holds the most titles is Palmeiras.

          Sofascore tracks live football scores and Brasileirão Série A table, results, statistics and top scorers.

          Competition format of Brasileirão Série A

          The teams are placed in 3 tables, with a promotion and relegation system for the best and worst-performing teams. 4 teams are relegated to the Brasileirão Série B.

          The top 4 team qualifies for the Copa Libertadores main tournament or preliminary qualifications.

          Additional 2 team qualify for Copa Libertadores Qualification tournament or preliminary qualifications.

          Additional 6 team qualify for Copa Sudamericana tournament or preliminary qualifications.

          Brasileirão Série A top scorers

          The top scorers in the last season of Brasileirão Série A Brasileirão Série A 2023 season were Germán Cano.Last season top scorer is Germán Cano with 26 goals.

          Team stadiums and attendance

          The team with the largest stadium is Flamengo, Fluminense with the capacity of 78838.

          The average attendance per game was 21652.

          The highest attendance per game in the season Brasileirão Série A 2023 was 55000.This was during the match between Atlético Mineiro and Flamengo.

          The total attendance in Brasileirão Série A 2023 season was 8163169.

          TV partners

          The largest TV partners for Brasileirão Série A are SporTV, Premiere, TV Globo.
